About Me

Welcome! I am Riya Negi, creator of Healthy Bites Website

Healthy Bites provides you a complete guide to manage you diet by providing delicious, nutrition packed recipies along with their health benifits.Whether you have food allergies or sensitivities or you just want to eat healthier, my goal is to provide you with lots of high-quality recipes to help you feel your best without compromising taste. From my experience, once you start eating home-cooked meals made with real food ingredients this website is not intended to solve health problems, but it's really meant to inspire you to cook more wholesome, real food recipes at home! and not a lot of processed foods, then you should start feeling better and having more energy.
