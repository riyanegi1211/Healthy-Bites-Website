Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Honey
  • Cinnamon
  • Dark chocolate
  • Old Fashioned rolled oats

Directions

  • In a small saucepan, combine peanut butter and honey.

  • Cook and stir over medium-low heat until melted and blended.

  • Remove from heat; stir in oats. Spread into a greased 9-in. square pan; press lightly.

  • Cool to room temperature; cover and chill for 1 hour.
