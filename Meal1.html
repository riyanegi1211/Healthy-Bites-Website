Vegetable Cheela

Cheela is one of the best dish for quick & easy breakfast. Here we bring a delicious vegetable
cheela recipe. You need just wheat flour, Indian spices and some veggies.

Nutritional Analysis Per Serving
Calories 94 calorie
Total Fat 3 gram
Fiber 2.6 gram
Cholestrol 0 gram
Protein 2.5 grams
Sugar 4 grams

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Atta
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 Haldi
  • 1/4 cups Dahi
  • 1/2 tsp Ajwain
  • Water, as required
  • 2-3 Green Chillies, chopped
  • 1 tbsp Beans and 2 tbsp Carrot , grated
  • 2 tbsp Onion
  • Handful of freshly chopped coriander leavess

Directions

  • Take atta, dahi, ajwain, salt and haldi in a mixing bowl.

  • Add curd and mix.

  • To it, water as requited and prepare a cheela batter.

  • Add all the vegetables and mix well.

  • Heat a tawa and grease with oil.

  • Add a ladleful of cheela batter on the tawa and spread well.

  • Cook both the sides and serve hot.
