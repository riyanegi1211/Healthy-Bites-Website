Mixed Berries & Banana Smoothie

This four-ingredient smoothie only takes a few minutes to blend and it serves two!
A smoothie with strawberries, blueberries and banana is delicately sweet and entirely
kid-friendly, even with a boost of protein from hemp seeds. Freeze the fruits ahead of
time for an extra frosty texture once blended.

Nutritional Analysis Per Serving
Calories 318 calorie
Total Fat 2.5 grams
Saturated Fat 1 grams
Chorestrol 6 milligrams
Sodium 83 milligrams
Carbohydrates 73 grams
Dietry Fiber 7 grams
Protein 9 grams
Sugar 53 grams

Ingredients

  • ½ cup frozen strawberries
  • ½ cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 small ripe banana (frozen, if desired)
  • ¾ cup chilled unsweetened cashew milk, plus more if needed
  • 1 tablespoon cashew butter
  • 1 tablespoon hulled hemp seeds

Direction

  • Combine strawberries, blueberries, banana, cashew milk, cashew butter and hemp seeds in a
    blender. Process until smooth, adding more cashew milk, if needed, for desired consistency.
    Serve immediately.
