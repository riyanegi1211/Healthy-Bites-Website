Kale Chips

One of the healthiest vegetables, kale is low in calories but high in water content, vitamins and
minerals. Munch on the chips by themselves or pair them with low-fat buttermilk ranch.

Nutritional Analysis Per Serving
Calories 190 calorie
Total Fat 10 grams
Carbohydrates 22 grams
Sodium 267 grams
Protein 8 grams
Sugar 1 grams

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch kale
  • Nonstick cooking spray
  • 1/2 tsp. Kosher salt

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

  • From kale, remove and discard thick stems, and tear leaves into large pieces.

  • Spread leaves in single layer on 2 large cookie sheets.

  • Spray leaves with nonstick cooking spray to coat lightly; sprinkle with salt.

  • Bake kale 12 to 15 minutes or just until kale chips are crisp but not browned.

  • Cool on cookie sheets on wire racks.
